Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our welding services

What on-site welding services do you provide? +
We offer structural steel repair, heavy-equipment welding, trailer & fleet maintenance, aluminum & stainless fabrication, and 24/7 emergency call-outs. Whether you need a crane boom fix or custom stainless railing, our rigs handle it all.
How do I request a quote? +
Fill out the contact form with project details and photos or call us. We’ll provide a free, no-obligation quote within 24 hours.
What areas do you serve and is there a travel fee? +
We’re based in St. Louis and cover a 50-mile radius; jobs beyond that may incur a small travel surcharge.
How long do repairs typically take? +
Small repairs take 1–2 hours; larger fabrications can take half a day or more. We’ll give you an estimated timeline when you request your quote.
What materials and processes do you work with? +
Certified in MIG, TIG, Stick, and Flux-Core welding on carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.
Are you insured and certified? +
Yes—full liability insurance, AWS certifications, and OSHA-30 training. Proof available on request.
