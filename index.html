Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers about our mobile welding services

What on-site welding services does Rudolph Mobile Welding provide? +
We offer structural steel repair, heavy-equipment welding, trailer & fleet maintenance, aluminum & stainless fabrication, and emergency 24/7 call-outs. Whether you need a crane boom fix or custom stainless railing, our mobile rigs handle it all.
How do I request a quote for a welding job? +
Simply fill out our contact form with project details and photos, or call us directly. We'll review your requirements, schedule an on-site visit if needed, and provide a free, no-obligation quote—usually within 24 hours.
What areas do you serve, and is there a travel fee? +
We’re based in St. Louis and cover a 50-mile radius for standard service. Jobs beyond that may incur a small travel surcharge. If you’re unsure, just ask—we’ll let you know upfront before you book.
How long does an on-site welding repair usually take? +
Most small repairs (e.g., reinforcing a beam or fixing a bracket) take 1–2 hours. Larger fabrications or equipment welds can take half a day or more. When you request a quote, we’ll give an estimated timeline tailored to your project.
What materials and welding processes do you work with? +
Our team is certified in MIG, TIG, Stick, and Flux-Core welding. We weld carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. Let us know your material and process preference (or ask for our recommendation) when you request service.
Is Rudolph Mobile Welding insured and certified? +
Yes. We carry full liability insurance and our welders hold AWS certifications as well as OSHA-30 safety training. We can provide proof of insurance or certificate copies upon request before work begins.
